# LoraLab Overview

LoraLab is a sophisticated AI content creation platform that Elios built from scratch. It enables users to generate and customize AI models, images, and videos. The platform leverages state-of-the-art machine learning technologies for:

1. Image generation
2. LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation) model training
3. Video creation from static images
4. Voice cloning

## Business Metrics

- 5,000+ active users across the platform
- $1,000+ Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- 100,000+ AI assets generated through the platform
- Strong user retention with 40% of users returning weekly
- Successfully launched API access for enterprise clients
- Processed transactions in both fiat and cryptocurrency
